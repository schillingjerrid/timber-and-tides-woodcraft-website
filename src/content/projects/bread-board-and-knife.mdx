## Overview

This bread board and knife set was designed as a cohesive gift piece. The board provides a generous surface for slicing and serving, while the knife handle was crafted to complement the board's natural character.

## Build Notes

- Face-grain maple board for a classic, clean look
- Routed hanging hole for easy storage
- Knife handle shaped and finished to match the board
- All food-contact surfaces treated with mineral oil and beeswax
- Edges eased with a light roundover for comfortable handling
