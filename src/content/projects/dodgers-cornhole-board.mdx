## Overview

Built for a diehard Dodgers fan, this set of regulation cornhole boards brings team pride to the backyard. The boards were built to official ACL tournament specs and overlayed with a custom Dodgers team logo for a polished, professional look. The solid walnut construct for the frame and bag stoarge make this a timeless piece that will handle the hard playstyle for years to come. 

## Build Notes

- Baltic birch plywood for a flat, stable playing surface
- Solid walnut frame and storage
- Regulation dimensions: 48in x 24in
- Legs fold flat for easy transport and storage
- Built-in French Cleat mount on the legs for easy wall storage
- Custom Dodgers graphics applied and sealed under multiple coats of exterior polyurethane
- Rounded corners and edges for a clean, safe finish

## Building Materials

- [Paper Birch](https://www.wood-database.com/paper-birch/) (Baltic Birch Plywood)
- [Black Walnut](https://www.wood-database.com/black-walnut/)
