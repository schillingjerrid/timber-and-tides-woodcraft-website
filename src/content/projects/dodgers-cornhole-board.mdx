## Overview

Built for a diehard Dodgers fan, this set of regulation cornhole boards brings team pride to the backyard. The boards were built to official ACL tournament specs and painted with Dodgers blue and the team logo for a polished, professional look.

## Build Notes

- Baltic birch plywood for a flat, stable playing surface
- Regulation dimensions: 48in x 24in with 6in hole centered 9in from top
- Legs fold flat for easy transport and storage
- Custom Dodgers graphics applied and sealed under multiple coats of exterior polyurethane
- Rounded corners and edges for a clean, safe finish
