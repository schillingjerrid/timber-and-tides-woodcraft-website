## Overview

This breadbox brings together traditional joinery and a warm cherry finish to create a kitchen staple that will last for generations. The roll-top tambour lid adds a satisfying tactile element to daily use.

## Build Notes

- Dovetailed case for strength and visual detail
- Tambour roll-top lid constructed from individual cherry slats
- Case back fitted with solid panel to allow for wood movement
- Finished with shellac for a warm tone, then paste wax for protection
- Interior left unfinished to remain food-safe

## Building Materials

- [Cherry](https://www.wood-database.com/black-cherry/)
