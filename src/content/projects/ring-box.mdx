## Overview

This compact ring box was made as a gift piece, designed to present a ring in a way that feels personal and handmade. The walnut brings a warm, earthy elegance, while the lined interior keeps the contents protected.

## Build Notes

- Solid walnut with book-matched lid panel
- Lid fitted with a snug, satisfying friction fit
- Interior lined with soft velvet for ring presentation
- Finished with Danish oil to enhance the grain, followed by paste wax
- Hand-sanded to a smooth, tactile finish throughout
