## Overview

This compact ring box was made as a gift piece, designed to present a ring with a personal and handmade touch. The walnut and cherry bring a warm, earthy elegance, while the doveltail sealed lid keeps the contents protected.

## Build Notes

- Walnut  and cherry construction
- Lid fitted with a snug, satisfying friction fit
- Finished with Danish oil to enhance the grain, followed by polyacrylic to give it a shine.
- Hand-sanded to a smooth finish throughout

## Building Materials

- [Black Walnut](https://www.wood-database.com/black-walnut/)
- [Cherry](https://www.wood-database.com/black-cherry/)
