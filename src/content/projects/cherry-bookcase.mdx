## Overview

This cherry bookcase was built as a commission for a home library. Cherry was chosen for its rich color that deepens beautifully over time with light exposure. The piece was designed to feel substantial and heirloom-quality while remaining functional for everyday use. 3 hidden drawers locked by RFID key readers secure personal belongings in a low-key safebox. 

## Build Notes

- Dadoed case with face frame for a strong structure without seeing any hardware 
- Hidden shelves integrate seamlessly into the bookcase
- Electronic key cards to unlock the shelves
- Classic look with a 21st-century upgrade
- Finished with Danish oil for a soft, warm texture that will hold its color for years
