## Overview

This cherry bookcase was built as a commission for a home library. Cherry was chosen for its rich color that deepens beautifully over time with light exposure. The piece was designed to feel substantial and heirloom-quality while remaining functional for everyday use.

## Build Notes

- Solid cherry throughout — no veneers or plywood
- Dadoed case with face frame for a traditional look
- Adjustable shelf pins allow flexible configuration
- Back panel is solid cherry to handle wood movement
- Finished with boiled linseed oil to pop the grain, then shellac for protection
- Top crown molding milled from solid cherry to match
