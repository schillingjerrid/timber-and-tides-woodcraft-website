## Overview

Every good shop needs good furniture. This collection of shop-built pieces — benches, carts, and storage fixtures — was designed around function first. Nothing fancy, but everything solid and well-thought-out for daily shop use.

## Build Notes

- Built primarily from construction-grade pine and shop-grade plywood
- Designed for rigidity and longevity under heavy use
- Features include drawers, shelving, and tool storage cutouts
- Left unfinished to keep things utilitarian and easy to modify
- All joinery is mechanical or glued-and-screwed for maximum strength

## Building Materials

- [Southern Yellow Pine](https://www.wood-database.com/longleaf-pine/)
