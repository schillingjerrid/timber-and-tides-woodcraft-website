## Overview

This Amish-inspired candy jar was crafted as a functional keepsake piece. The design draws on traditional forms while using premium hardwood for a timeless result that looks as good on a shelf as it does on a kitchen counter.

## Build Notes

- Turned from solid walnut stock
- Fitted lid with a snug friction fit
- Interior and exterior finished with food-safe mineral oil
- Hand-sanded through progressive grits for a silky smooth surface
