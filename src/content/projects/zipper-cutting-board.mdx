## Overview

The zipper pattern on this end-grain board creates a visual illusion of interlocking teeth running the length of the board. The sharp contrast between light maple and dark walnut makes the pattern pop, turning a functional kitchen tool into a piece of art.

## Build Notes

- End-grain construction for a self-healing, knife-friendly surface
- Two-species design: hard maple and black walnut for maximum contrast
- Zipper pattern achieved through precise strip cutting and offset glue-up
- Flattened on a router sled to ensure perfectly level surface
- Finished with food-safe mineral oil and beeswax, buffed to a low sheen
- Recessed grip handles on both sides for easy lifting
