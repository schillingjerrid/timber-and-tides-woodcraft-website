## Overview

This whiskey board was designed for the serious spirits enthusiast. The board provides a dedicated surface for a whiskey flight alongside cheeses, meats, and accompaniments, with routed recesses to keep glasses stable and positioned perfectly.

## Build Notes

- Solid walnut for rich color and natural antibacterial properties
- Routed circular recesses sized for standard whiskey glasses
- Live edge retained on one side for natural character
- Generous board length for a full charcuterie spread
- All surfaces finished with food-safe mineral oil and beeswax blend
- Hanging hole routed for wall storage between uses
