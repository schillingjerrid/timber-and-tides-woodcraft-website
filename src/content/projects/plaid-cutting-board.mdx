## Overview

This plaid cutting board takes inspiration from classic tartan patterns, translating them into wood grain using three complementary species. The contrast between light maple, rich walnut, and warm cherry creates a vibrant, eye-catching design that's also a workhorse in the kitchen.

## Build Notes

- End-grain construction for self-healing knife surface
- Three species: hard maple, black walnut, and cherry
- Multiple glue-up stages to achieve the plaid grid pattern
- Flattened on a router sled, then hand-sanded through 220 grit
- Finished with several coats of food-safe mineral oil and beeswax
