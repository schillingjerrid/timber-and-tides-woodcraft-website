## Overview

The basket weave pattern on this cutting board is achieved by carefully gluing and re-cutting strips of alternating maple and walnut, then rotating and re-laminating them to create the interlocking woven appearance. The result is a board that's as striking as it is functional.

## Build Notes

- End-grain construction for knife-friendly surface
- Maple and walnut strips alternated for high contrast pattern
- Multiple glue-up stages to achieve the woven effect
- Finished with multiple coats of food-safe mineral oil and beeswax blend
- Juice groove routed around the perimeter
